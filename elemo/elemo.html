Elemo passenger elevator employ advanced technology in vertical transportation. Passenger Lift is used to carry a maximum of 26 passengers. We provide geared, gearless and machine room less Elevator.

Gearless Lifts(MRL)

Machine room-less elevators are designed so that most of the components fit within the shaft containing the elevator car; and a small cabinet houses the elevator controller. Other than the machinery being in the hoistway, the equipment is similar to a normal traction elevator. These elevators create more usable space and are more energy efficient. We are supplying MRL lifts with a speed 1mtr/Sec. to 1.6mtr/Sec. with a load of upto 16 passengers
